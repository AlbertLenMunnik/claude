# NexusCheck — Brand Identity

## Logo
The NexusCheck logo combines a gradient shield icon with a clean wordmark. The "N" inside the shield represents protection and compliance, while the crosshair dot signals precision and detection.

### Logo Files
- `logo.svg` — Primary logo (dark background)
- `logo-light-bg.svg` — For light backgrounds
- `logo-icon.svg` — Icon only (app icons, favicons, social)

## Voice & Tone

**Positioning:** The trusted, no-BS sales tax compliance tool for online sellers.

- **Clear over clever.** People are stressed about tax compliance. Don't make jokes — make it simple.
- **Urgent but not alarming.** State the risk factually. Let the numbers create the urgency.
- **Authoritative but accessible.** Write like a sharp accountant who speaks plain English.
- **Action-oriented.** Every page ends with a clear next step.

### Example Copy
- Good: "You have nexus in 7 states. Here's what to do next."
- Bad: "Uh oh! Looks like you might owe some taxes! 😱"
- Good: "Since the 2018 Wayfair ruling, online sellers can owe sales tax with zero physical presence."
- Bad: "Tax laws are super confusing but don't worry we'll help!"
