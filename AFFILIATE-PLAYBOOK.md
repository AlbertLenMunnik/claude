# NexusCheck — Affiliate & Partner Playbook

## Priority Order (apply to all of these)

### 1. Avalara — Referral Partner Program ✅ PRIORITY
- **Apply here:** https://www.avalara.com/us/en/partners/partner-programs.html
- **Program type:** Referral Partner (lowest barrier to entry)
- **What they pay:** 15–20% of first-year subscription revenue per referred customer
- **Their plans start at:** ~$200/mo → your payout: $360–480 per conversion
- **Approval time:** 1–2 weeks
- **What they want to see:** A live website with real traffic + a clear distribution channel
- **Action:** Apply through the portal. Use NexusCheck website as your "channel."

### 2. Kintsugi — Partner Program ✅ STRONG FIT
- **Apply here:** https://trykintsugi.com/partners
- **Why them:** AI-native sales tax platform, actively growing, hungry for distribution
- **Program includes:** Revenue sharing, dedicated Slack support, co-marketing, partner dashboard
- **Best fit because:** They're a newer player who will value a referral channel more than Avalara does. Expect better terms and more attention.
- **Action:** Apply through partner page. Mention NexusCheck's high-intent traffic.

### 3. Zamp — Referral / White-Label Partner ✅ STRONG FIT
- **Apply here:** https://zamp.com (look for "Become a Partner" or contact partnerships team)
- **Contact:** support@zamp.com or 1-866-438-9267
- **Why them:** Fully managed service (they handle everything), great for SMB ecommerce. They actively want CPA and platform referral partners.
- **Action:** Email their partnerships team directly (draft below).

### 4. Anrok — Advisory Partner / Customer Referral ⚡ GOOD FIT
- **Apply here:** https://www.anrok.com/partners/advisory
- **Referral program:** https://www.anrok.com/customer-referral
- **Why them:** Built for SaaS companies specifically. If NexusCheck traffic includes SaaS sellers (it will), Anrok is the right referral.
- **Action:** Apply through partner page.

### 5. TaxJar / Stripe Tax — ❌ DEPRIORITISE
- **Status:** TaxJar killed their independent affiliate program in April 2024
- **Current path:** You'd need to go through Stripe's partner ecosystem, which is slow and enterprise-focused
- **Recommendation:** Skip for now. Revisit when you have meaningful volume (500+ referrals/month).

## Tracking Setup

When affiliate links are live, tag each one with UTM parameters:

```
https://partner-url.com/?ref=nexuscheck&utm_source=nexuscheck&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=checker_results
```

Also track these events in Google Analytics:
- `checker_completed` — user finished the nexus check
- `results_viewed` — results page loaded
- `affiliate_click_{provider}` — clicked through to a provider
- `affiliate_cta_visible` — recommendations section was shown (scroll tracking)
